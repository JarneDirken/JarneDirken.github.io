Introduction

Choosing the right font is hugely important. A font supports the message you want to deliver to the reader in 2 ways. On the one hand, a font that is easy to read is necessary for the content of your site to penetrate the surfer properly. On the other hand, the chosen font accentuates who the target audience of your site is.

For a wide target audience, always choose sans serif letters. These can be generic sans serif fonts such as Arial or Verdana, but thanks to Google Fonts there is a much wider range of sans serif webfonts with which you can make even more of a difference.

On a site, use no more than 2 different fonts. For the majority of texts, choose an easy-to-read typeface. Only for the titles you can consider a slightly more artistic font, possibly even a font with serifs or a font that looks handwritten. This is often done on sites where luxury goods are offered.
